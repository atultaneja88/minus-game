How to Play

🎯Goal: Lowest cumulative score after 10 rounds wins.
🃏Cards: K=14, Q=12, J=0, A=1, numbers = face value.
①Step 1: Select a card from your hand and discard it.
②Step 2: Draw from the hidden deck OR take the top discard.
🔁Duplicates: Discard all cards of same value at once, then draw one.
⚡SHOW: Call at turn start (before discarding). Ends round. +25 penalty if you're not lowest!
