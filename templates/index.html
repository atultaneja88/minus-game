How to Play

🎯Goal: Lowest cumulative score after 10 rounds wins.
🃏Cards: K=14, Q=12, J=0, A=1, numbers = face value.
①Step 1: Select a card from your hand and discard it.
②Step 2: Draw from deck OR take the previous player's discard.
🔁Duplicates: Discard all same-value cards at once, then draw one.
⚡SHOW: Call before discarding. +25 penalty if not lowest!
